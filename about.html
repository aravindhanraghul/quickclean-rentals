About QuickClean Rentals

Who We Are

QuickClean Rentals is a locally-run rental service in Whitefield, Bangalore. We provide high-quality home cleaning equipment like electric mops, robotic mops, and vacuum cleaners on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

Our mission is to make modern cleaning tools accessible to every household and business without the high cost of ownership. With a focus on hygiene, customer support, and reliability, QuickClean is trusted by hundreds of happy renters.
